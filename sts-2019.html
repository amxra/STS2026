About This Edition

The 2019 Sustainability Table Discourse Series convened leading experts and stakeholders to explore actionable pathways for sustainable development in Nigeria. The discussions highlighted how finance, food systems, gender equality, and the creative industries intersect within the Green Economy.

A key focus was climate financing and the green capital shift, stressing sustainable finance as the engine for social and environmental impact. Panelists urged Nigerian financial institutions to adopt the Nigerian Sustainable Banking Principles and pivot from fossil fuels to renewable energy.

In food security, climate change was recognized as an immediate threat. Experts advocated for modern ranching, precision farming, greenhouse technology, and climate-resilient crops, as well as reducing post-harvest losses through localized processing.

Gender equality was highlighted as a critical driver of green growth. The series called for dismantling structural inequalities, promoting women in leadership and STEM fields, and also explored sustainable fashion and the circular economy in creative industries.
