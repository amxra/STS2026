About This Edition

STS 2022 focused on the transformative role of technology and digital infrastructure in driving sustainable development and circular economy principles. The event highlighted the immense potential of broadband and digital technology to bridge the digital divide.

Key Takeaway

"Digital skills development, robust infrastructure, and regulatory support are the three pillars needed to enable broader participation in Nigeria's digital economy."

Key discussions stressed the need for digital skills development, robust infrastructure, and regulatory support. Stakeholders emphasized digital literacy initiatives through partnerships between governments, educational institutions, and the private sector.

Panel discussions explored expanding broadband coverage especially in underserved regions. Public-private partnerships were seen as essential to foster innovation and ensure sustainable digital infrastructure.

Closing thoughts emphasized broadband technology as a powerful enabler for economic inclusion, access to essential services, and entrepreneurial innovation.
