About STS

A platform facilitating the dialogue Africa needs and the action sustainable development demands.

The Sustainability Table Discourse Series (STS) brings together policymakers, business leaders, civil society, the academic community, development partners and innovators to address critical challenges across environmental, social and governance priorities.

Since 2016, STS has grown from a focused roundtable into a regionally recognised annual summit, influencing public discourse and shaping policy aligned with the UN SDGs, COP outcomes, and continental development frameworks.

Past Editions

From a focused roundtable to a continental movement.

2025
STS 2025 · 9th Edition

Sustainability in Action: Scaling Impact for a Thriving Future to Achieve the SDGs

The 9th edition convened 842 participants and 15 global speakers, five years from the 2030 SDGs deadline. The summit marked a shift from ambition to execution, framing sustainability as Africa's most strategic economic pathway and reaffirming the Tripartite Collaboration Model.

2024
STS 2024 · 8th Edition

Navigating Challenges, Leveraging Opportunities in a Reforming Economy to Achieve the SDGs

The 8th edition convened over 300 stakeholders to address sustainable development in Nigeria's evolving economy. Discussions centred the Orange Economy, ESG integration, circular models, and PPPs, closing with strategic recommendations on MSME support and policy reform.

2023
STS 2023 · 7th Edition

Mobilising Finance for the SDGs: Expanding Green Finance in Times of Global Crisis

Leaders convened to unlock finance for the SDGs amid global headwinds. Discussion spotlighted green finance as a $12 trillion opportunity, addressed the $2.5 trillion annual gap, and explored blended finance, DFIs, green bonds, pension capital and federal climate policy frameworks.
