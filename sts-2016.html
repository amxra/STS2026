About This Edition

The 2016 edition of The Sustainability Table Discourse Series, themed "The Green Table – Opportunities in a Green Economy," focused on recalibrating economies from being resource-based to knowledge-based.

Key Takeaway

"A shift toward a green economy requires not just the rethinking of industries, but the empowering of people with knowledge and technology to lead this transformative process."

It highlighted the immense potential of a green economy, emphasizing the need to adopt sustainable practices that not only preserve environmental resources but also foster innovation, create new job opportunities, and drive economic growth.

This summit served as a critical platform for engaging thought leaders, policy makers, and industry experts in discussions about the future of sustainable business practices and how economies can transition to a more sustainable model.
