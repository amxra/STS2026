About This Edition

STS 2021 focused on strategies for sustainable job creation and waste management within a circular economy framework. Discussions emphasized aligning Nigeria's economic growth with sustainability practices that foster environmental stewardship, resource efficiency, and long-term prosperity.

The first panel, "Sustainable Job Creation for Economic Prosperity in a Circular Economy," examined how circular economy practices can drive economic growth through job creation, skill development, and private-sector involvement.

The second panel, "Effective Waste Management and Pollution Control in a Circular Economy," tackled responsible waste management solutions and the role of government regulations, private-sector partnerships, and community engagement.

Throughout the event, panelists drew from their diverse backgrounds, sharing practical insights and stressing the urgency of adopting circular economy principles to address Nigeria's environmental and economic challenges.
